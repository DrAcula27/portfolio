About Me

I'm based in the greater Seattle, WA area working in a remote team.
When I'm not coding, I can be found chasing and playing with my dog, reading all the books, tasting new teas, and giving various hobbies a whirl (most recently gardening). I push code frequently; feel free to connect with me on GitHub: Github

Building and enhancing software to improve everyone's lives is what I do. Passion for learning and improvement has always been part of me. In university, I studied engineering intending to make processes simpler, smoother, and more efficient. While working across diverse industries I was struck by any improvement, no matter how small, made radical impacts on customer happiness and satisfaction. Between work and pursuing my own outside interests, I have seen just how much the right technology can help people every day. Now, I devote my time to building those products and improving the world a little bit at a time.
